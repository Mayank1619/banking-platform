# Quickstart: Validate the Dark Neon Theme Update

1. Open agreed customer and admin pages and confirm the global dark navy/black surfaces are applied.
2. Verify primary, secondary, and destructive actions render with distinct default, hover, focus, active, disabled, and loading states.
3. Check forms, alerts, tables, badges, progress bars, empty states, and loading states for the shared theme.
4. Confirm accessibility cues remain visible for warning, error, and success states without relying on colour alone.
5. Re-run existing user journeys to confirm no functional behavior or visibility rules changed.
